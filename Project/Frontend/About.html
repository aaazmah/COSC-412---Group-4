About us

Results of a recent study commissioned by the US Dept of Health and Human Services (or DHHS) has revealed that the general population is largely unaware that proper nutrition and exercise are vital to overall good health. Furthermore, the study revealed that the average person is more likely to develop a sustained interest in physical activity and a healthy lifestyle if they are provided with some type of interactive, technology-based tool to stimulate their interest in the areas. Rapidly increasing public interest in developing sustainable, healthy lifestyles has led the DHHS to form the Healthy Lifestyles (or HL) committee. The goal of this committee will be managing the creation of a technology-based gaming tool to meet this need. The HL Committee made the decision to engage Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) from various related industries including fitness, medical, health/nutrition and technology, to determine the initial needs of the product and lead this effort to fruition.
